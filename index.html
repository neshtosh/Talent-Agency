For talent.

We are passionate about discovering and nurturing Kenyan talent. We are here to represent you, connect you with opportunities both locally and internationally, and ensure that you’re treated with the professionalism and respect you deserve. Whether you’re an emerging artist or a seasoned performer, VAT is committed to growing your career, helping you find your audience, and supporting you through every stage of your journey.

  • We invest in talent, offering representation and growth opportunities.
  • VAT ensures transparency in contracts, payments, and management.
  • We connect artists with globally, expanding their reach beyond borders.




for agencies.

We are your trusted partner in East Africa. We offer seamless and reliable artist management, ensuring that your artists are well-represented and supported on the ground. With our deep understanding of the region’s culture, industry, and logistics, VAT guarantees professionalism and efficiency in every collaboration. We strive to be an extension of your agency, upholding your values and ensuring the highest standards of care for your artists. Moreover, our partnership with Homeboyz Entertainment, a leader in the Kenyan events industry, amplifies our capabilities and networks, giving you confidence in our ability to deliver exceptional results.

  • VAT acts as the regional representative for international agencies, ensuring smooth event operations in East Africa.
  • We offer transparency, reliability, and a deep understanding of local markets.
  • Our collaboration with Homeboyz Entertainment enhances our reach and resources, ensuring that the artists you send to East Africa are treated with the same respect and care you provide in your home markets.




for promoters.

We are here to work with promoters to ensure the success of every event. We offer a curated roster of high-quality talent, from DJs to bands, and guarantee that our artists will meet the highest standards of professionalism. VAT is committed to fostering partnerships with promoters to create memorable experiences for audiences across East Africa. Our collaboration with Homeboyz Entertainment, a well-established name in the industry, further enhances our ability to deliver exceptional talent and production support. Let’s collaborate to elevate events and showcase the best of what the region has to offer.

  • VAT offers a diverse range of artists, from emerging local talent to well-established names.
  • We ensure professional and seamless artist management, allowing promoters to focus on the success of their event.
  • Our partnership with Homeboyz Entertainment strengthens our position in the market, enabling us to provide comprehensive support and access to top talent for your events.
